# Image Attribution

Every demo vehicle image used in this repository, with its source and licence.
All are licensed for reuse. No manufacturer marketing photography and no dealer
listing photos are used.

Images are stored locally and read from disk — nothing is fetched at runtime, so the
demo works with no network.

## Licence summary

| Licence | Files | Attribution required |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CC BY 4.0 | 1 | Yes |
| CC0 | 4 | No |
| CC0 1.0 | 6 | No |
| Public domain | 1 | No |

CC0 and public-domain files were preferred wherever a suitable one existed: no
attribution obligation and no share-alike is the least encumbered footing for imagery
shown to a customer. Credit is recorded here for every file regardless.

## Preparation

Each original was downscaled to at most 1600px wide and re-encoded as progressive
JPEG (quality 86). EXIF metadata was dropped. No cropping, retouching, or colour
adjustment was applied — the cards crop at render time via `object-fit: cover`.

## Accuracy note

These are stand-ins for merchandising photography, not records of specific vehicles.
Several differ from the fixture in trim or model year while matching the body and
generation — for example V-10009 is a 2017 Ram 1500 Rebel in the data and a Ram 1500
of the same generation in the photograph. In production `image_url` would carry the
actual photograph of the actual car.

## Fallback artwork

Vehicles without a usable `image_url` fall back to a generated body-style silhouette
in `ui_components.py`. That artwork is original to this repository and carries no
third-party rights.
